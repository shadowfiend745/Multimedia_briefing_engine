INTRO
Arknights: Endfield is a real-time 3D action RPG spinoff developed by Hypergryph that launched on January 22, 2026, combining combat, exploration, and factory-building mechanics on a colonized moon.

KEY POINTS
• The game features a combo skill system where players control a four-member team simultaneously, switching between characters to use tactical skills, basic attacks, and ultimate abilities while managing shared skill gauges.

• A factory-building system inspired by games like Factorio allows players to harvest resources, refine materials, and route them through conveyor belts, with developers aiming for a 50:50 balance between RPG and building gameplay.

• The story takes place on moon Talos-II, colonized 152 years prior by Terran pioneers, where players take the role of "Endministrator" managing Endfield Industries while facing threats like the supernatural "Corruption" disaster and hostile creatures called "Aggeloi."

• Reviews praised the game's excellent visual presentation, art style, and cyberpunk atmosphere, but criticized repetitive combat, dragging narrative pacing, and complex menu systems that detract from overall depth.

• The game released across multiple platforms (PC, iOS, Android, PS5) and achieved over 35 million pre-registrations worldwide, ranking 2nd on iOS App Store charts on its first day.

• Critical reception was mostly positive (78/100 on Metacritic, 88% recommend on OpenCritic), though opinions varied on whether the ambitious systems deliver meaningful gameplay or remain superficially complex.

SUMMARY
Arknights: Endfield is an ambitious action RPG that uniquely blends real-time combat, open-world exploration, and deep factory-building systems on a lunar colony setting. While reviewers consistently praised its visual design and innovative system combinations, concerns about narrative pacing, combat repetitiveness, and whether the complexity serves meaningful progression divided critical opinion on its overall execution.
